On the way to Devoxx

06 December 2008

I really look forward to this as conferences are always a good change to sniff the latest news and currents as well as meeting a lot of fellow developers. Furthermore I will give a short intro into Hades on Tuesday.

I will try to sum up every day with a small blog post as I did on SpringOne Europe already.
