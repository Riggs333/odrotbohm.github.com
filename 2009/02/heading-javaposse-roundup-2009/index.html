Heading Java Posse Roundup 2009

26 February 2009

2 more days and I will make my way to Crested Butte, Colorado to join this year’s Java Posse Roundup. Not having attended an “Uncoference” before I really look forward to this style of conference as the concept sounds promising. The week will be packed with geek activities but also leave room for leisure as Crested Butte is situated in a large skiing area actually. Last year’s pictures look awesome.

I’ll try to post here now and then to reflect what’s going on over there but i guess Twitter frequency will be much higher.
